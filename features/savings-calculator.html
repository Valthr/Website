Assumptions & sources
Manual delivery duration
25 minutes per round trip across the 2.5 × 2 km refinery, covering loading, logging and return. Illustrative estimate based on typical industrial logistics workflows.
Time spent in ATEX classified zones per manual trip
15 minutes per trip, or 60% of the total delivery time. Conservative estimate, reflecting that most pickup and delivery points sit within classified process areas.
Technician hourly cost
£8.69 per hour. Derived from the £15,360 annual Bahrain petroleum technician salary (report section 7.2), divided by 1,768 working hours per year.
Working year for a full time technician
1,768 hours per year: 221 working days of 8 hours, after 10 public holidays and 30 standard leave days (report ABC model).
Manual baseline vehicle
Toyota Hilux pickup. Diesel consumption of 10 L per 100 km, emission factor of 0.268 kg CO₂ per km (UK DEFRA 2024 conversion factor for diesel), over a 2 km average round trip within the refinery.
Drone electricity consumption per mission
0.8 kWh per mission. Derived from the ABC model: 17,680 kWh per year across the fleet, divided by 22,100 missions per year (Appendix 1).
Bahrain grid carbon intensity
0.49 kg CO₂ per kWh, based on the 2020 national emission factor (Ember, published via Statista and the IEA). Bahrain's grid is almost entirely natural gas.
Valthr total cost per mission
£12.86 per mission, from the ABC model in report section 7.3. This comprises £2.57 of amortised CAPEX and £10.29 of OPEX.
Tree CO₂ equivalence
21 kg CO₂ absorbed per year by one mature tree (US EPA Greenhouse Gas Equivalencies reference).
